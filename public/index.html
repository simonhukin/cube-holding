About

Cube is a Sydney-based independent public relations agency working in health and nutrition. At Cube, we believe effective communication requires careful research, creative tactics and quality execution.

Our goal is to set new standards in healthcare communications - driving innovative campaign development and higher benchmarks in implementation and service delivery.

From creative consumer communications to professional education, to brand reputation, to stakeholder development, our commitment is to build tailored communication strategies that meet business goals.
