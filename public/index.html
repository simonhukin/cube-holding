About Cube

Cube was established in 2004 by Managing Director, Anne-Marie Sparrow. Cube is a Sydney-based independent public relations agency working in the specialties of health and nutrition. At Cube, we believe effective communications require considered research, strategic insight, creative and implementable tactics, and quality execution.

Our goal is to set new standards in healthcare communications - driving innovative campaign development and higher benchmarks in implementation and service delivery.

From creative consumer communications to professional education, from brand reputation to stakeholder development, our commitment is to build tailored communications strategies that address your business needs and meet objectives.
